How It Works

  1. Create Your Love List

    Start by selecting up to 10 individuals of the opposite gender based on your preferences. Browse through profiles, check out interests, and curate your personalized Love List.

  2. Express Your Interest

    Once you've compiled your Love List, express your interest by ranking your selections according to your preferences. Higher rankings indicate a stronger interest, so don't be shy – let your heart guide you!

  3. Wait for the Magic

    After the selection period, the magic happens behind the scenes. Our cutting-edge matchmaking algorithm goes to work, analyzing preferences and compatibility to create the most promising matches.

  4. The Big Reveal

    On the designated date, we unveil the results! If you and someone from your Love List have both ranked each other highly, congratulations – you've found a match made in puppy match heaven.

  5. Connect and Explore

    Once the matches are revealed, you can connect with your potential valentines, explore their profiles, and kickstart the conversation. It's time to turn virtual connections into real-world sparks!
